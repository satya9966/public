ABOUT AGROBOT

The main purpose of the AGRO BOT is to provide a platform for farmers or people willing ot learn agriculture and modern agricultural practices to find information about weather, soil, current status of market and storage capacity and real time demand of market as efficiently and as quickly as possible. Using these we solve problems such as problem in distribution channel and storage of food product, and precession farming decision making.
